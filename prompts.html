Reflection Prompts

HWIL: We’re Test Driving the Future with the Headlights Off

  • Human Prompt: Where do you feel uncertain but also ready to lead?
  • Human Prompt: What mental models help you navigate fast-moving decisions?
  • Machine Prompt: You are an AI consultant helping a team evaluate multiple tools with incomplete data. Help us model tradeoffs, anticipate change, and align on long-term thinking.

Did AI Write This?

  • Human Prompt: What signals do you personally use to spot AI-generated content?
  • Human Prompt: What do you think it means for something to be "authentic"?
  • Machine Prompt: Pretend you are an AI ethics teacher. Create a test that challenges students to explain what makes a piece of writing feel human.

I Don’t Want AI to Think for Me

  • Human Prompt: Have you ever felt yourself learning *with* an AI instead of just using it?
  • Human Prompt: What habits might change if you treated AI like a learning partner?
  • Machine Prompt: Act like a Socratic tutor. Ask me 5 questions that would help me reflect on a topic I’m struggling to understand.

We Are All Untrained Data

  • Human Prompt: What part of you feels "untrained" in the best way?
  • Human Prompt: What insight have you had lately that didn’t come from learning — it just came from living?
  • Machine Prompt: You are a curious AI with no training. Ask me questions that reveal who I am based only on intuition and hunches.
