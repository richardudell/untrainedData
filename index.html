About Untrained Data

Untrained Data is a personal writing experiment and public demonstration: can a human stay human while creating with machines?

Everything here was shaped with help from AI — not hidden, but highlighted. Drafts, structures, edits, design — all accelerated by large language models. But the ideas, the direction, and the final judgment? That’s me.

Sometimes, I write solo. Sometimes, I riff with a model. Sometimes I reflect with it like a friend. I label it all using a UTD token system to show how a piece was made:

  • UTD-1: Pure voice (no AI)
  • UTD-2: AI-assisted, edited by me
  • UTD-3: Co-created with AI
  • UTD-X: Undefined process

Untrained doesn’t mean unfinished. It means unbounded. Liberated. Still learning. It’s a reminder that insight doesn’t only come from training data — sometimes it comes from a feeling, a riff, a pause.
